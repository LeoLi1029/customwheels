About CustomRim Works

At **CustomRim Works**, we don’t just sell wheels – we **engineer** them. With our **own factory**, we handle every step of the process: from **designing**, performing **structural calculations**, to **manufacturing**, **painting**, and **final quality checks**. Our mission is to **craft high-performance custom wheels** that combine **durability, style, and cutting-edge technology**.

Innovative Wheel Design

Our in-house design team uses the latest **3D modeling** and **structural analysis software** to create wheels that are both stylish and aerodynamic.

Precision Engineering & Testing

Each wheel undergoes **stress testing, impact testing, and load distribution analysis** to ensure it meets the highest industry standards.

Manufacturing Excellence

Our **forging and casting process** ensures that each wheel is built for performance and longevity. We use **state-of-the-art CNC machines** to achieve the highest precision.

Custom Painting & Finishing

Choose from **custom powder coating, polished finishes, or unique designs**. Our advanced painting facility ensures a **durable, high-gloss finish**.
